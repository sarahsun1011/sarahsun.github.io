What's in the Dungeon?






This is a VR horror world that is also compatible with PC made with Unity VRChat SDK. This world is also playable on VRChat.
Controls:
- Thumbsticks of the VR controllers to move
- Pull the trigger to interact
Gameplay:
Stare at the monster so that it doesn't harm you! To fully stop the monster, you also need to put off all the fire to stop performing the ritual.
