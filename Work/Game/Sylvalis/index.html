Sylvalis






Sylvalis is my thesis at NYU. It is a multiplayer VR social game made with Unity aiming to reduce toxic behaviors in game.
Precise hand tracking can be used while playing to create a more immersive experience.
Controls:
- Hand tracking: right hand pinch to teleport or select
- Joystick: right hand thumbstick to move, left hand thumbstick to rotate, trigger to select.
- Prevention system: Reacts to aggressive hand gesture immediately, results in a negative gaming experience for the trollers.
Gameplay:
Collabrate with your friends to explore the forest and use your love to help animals! Let's create your own story in this journey!
