Ecological Simulator






This is a group project of two people, and also the first game that I've ever made. The game is to portrait a lovely fictional life of a fish under the water.
This project can be categorized into two parts: The game, and the console. The entire game is made with Processing, and its controls are made with buttons and potentiometers with Arduino.
Roles:
Game Design & Concept Design: Sarah Sun & Xiaozao Wang
Game development: Xiaozao Wang
Console Design & Console Development: Sarah Sun
