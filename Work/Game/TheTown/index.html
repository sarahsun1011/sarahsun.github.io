Centipede






This is a group project making an eerie story-driven adventure game. The game was inspired by Sally Face, and we chose to use a similar hand-drawn style as our art style.
roles:
Game Design: Sarah Sun & Katrina Ramirez
Visuals: Katrina Ramirez
Game Development: Sarah Sun
Sound: Sarah Sun
Controls:
- Use mouse to control where to go and click to interact
Gameplay:
Talk to the people in the village and try to reveal the dark secret behind the town without being caught!
