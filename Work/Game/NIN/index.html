Nightmare in NeverLand






This is a VR horror game that is inspired by Alice in Wonderland. Made with Unity.
Roles:
Level 1: Ana Eremina
Level 2: Simran Chhabra
Hand Tracking & Level 3: Sarah Sun
Controls:
- Right hand pinch to teleporte
-Left hand pinch and swipe to rotate
Gameplay:
Follow the guide in game and enjoy this eerie experience!
