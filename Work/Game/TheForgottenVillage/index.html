The Forgotten Village






This is my senior project at NYU. It is a 3D single player open world horror game.
The game consists of 1 animation and 4 levels.
The beginning animation, shows how did the player get to this village
Village, finding missing journal pages and collect them to gather information. Based on the number of pages are found, players will be teleported to different levels that shows the memories behind the journal.
Maze, shows the horrorible human experiments in the memory that were operated here.
Puzzle, a picture of the slaughter in the memory
Final boss fight, all secrets are reveal, it is time to fight. But is what you saw what you think?
Tools:
C4D for rendering the animation, Reaper for sound editting, Unity for game design and game development
Controls:
- WASD to move
- Space to jump
- E to interact
- mouse to pan
Gameplay:
Village, collect journals and read them. They can be rechecked in the inventory
Maze, find the way out without being caught by the patrolling enemies. Hide in lockers if necessary
Puzzle, finish the puzzle before the timer
Final boss fight, use different weapons to fight against the final boss! Be sure to dodge his attack!
