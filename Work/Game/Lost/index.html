Lost






This is a 3D split-screen multiplayer maze game made with Unity.
Roles:
Game Design: Sarah Sun & Katrina Ramirez
Level Design: Katrina Ramirez
Game Development: Sarah Sun
Controls:
- WASD to move, E to interact for player 1
- Up Down Left Right arrows to move, right Shift to interact for player 2
Gameplay:
Players will be separated at the beginning of the game. Their goal is to help each other to pass through all obstacles, and eventually reunite.
